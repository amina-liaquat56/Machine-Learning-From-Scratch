Introduction to Differential Privacy (DP)

===================================================

Definition
==================================

Differential Privacy is a system for publicly sharing information about a dataset by describing patterns of groups within the dataset while withholding information about individuals in the dataset.
In simple terms: It allows us to analyze data without compromising anyone's personal privacy.

Importance
=================================
Protects individual data even if someone has access to the dataset.

Used by tech companies like Apple, Google, and Microsoft to enhance data privacy in analytics.

Helps comply with data protection laws such as GDPR and HIPAA.
