       Applications of ML
======================================

Applications                        Descriptions                                                               ML Types

Recommendation Systems         Netflix suggests movies, Amazon suggests products.                         Supervised (Collaborative Filtering)
Image Recognition              Facebook tags people in photos, self-driving cars detect pedestrians.      Supervised (Classification)
Spam Filtering                 Gmail automatically filters junk mail.                                     Supervised (Classification)
Voice Assistants               Siri, Alexa, and Google Assistant understand and respond to speech.        Supervised (NLP)
Fraud Detection                Credit card companies flag suspicious transactions.                        Supervised (Anomaly Detection)
Customer Segmentation          Grouping customers based on purchasing behavior.                           Unsupervised (Clustering)
Medical Diagnosis              Assisting doctors in identifying diseases from scans.                      Supervised (Classification)
